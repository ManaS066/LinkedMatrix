Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about LinkedMatrix

How do I join as an alumnus?

To join as an alumnus, simply click on "Alumni Sign Up" and complete your profile with your graduation year, field of expertise, and current position. You can then choose how you'd like to contribute‚Äîwhether by posting opportunities, mentoring students, or participating in events.

Is LinkedMatrix free for students?

Yes! LinkedMatrix is completely free for students. We believe in making career connections accessible to all students. Simply register with your university email address to verify your student status and gain access to all platform features.

How do I post an opportunity for students?

As an alumnus, once you've created your profile, you can post opportunities by navigating to the "Post Opportunity" section in your dashboard. Fill out the details including position, requirements, application deadline, and whether it's an internship, full-time role, or project. You can choose to make it visible to students from specific departments or all students.

Can I connect with alumni from specific industries?

Absolutely! As a student, you can filter alumni by industry, company, graduation year, or area of expertise. Our advanced search feature helps you find the perfect mentors or connections based on your career interests and goals.

How does the mentorship program work?

Our mentorship program matches students with alumni based on shared interests, career goals, and expertise. Alumni specify their mentoring capacity and areas of guidance, while students indicate what they're looking for in a mentor. Once matched, you can schedule meetings, set goals, and track progress through our platform.

Are there networking events on LinkedMatrix?

Yes! We regularly host both virtual and in-person networking events. Alumni can create or sponsor events, while students can browse and register for events relevant to their interests. These include industry panels, resume workshops, mock interviews, and casual networking mixers.
